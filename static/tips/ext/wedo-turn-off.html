Turn motors or lights off.

Turn the lights on for 2 seconds when the space key is pressed, then turn them off.


You can select a LEGO-WeDo-compatible device from the dropdown menu:
  • If you have only one motor, use the "motor" option, which will turn the motor off regardless of where it is connected on the WeDo hub.
  • If you have two motors which you want to control separately, you can use "motor A" with one and "motor B" with the other.
  • "lights" turn the lights off regardless of where they are connected on the WeDo hub.
  • "everything" turns off all the connected motors and lights.

To use turn off, you need WeDo motor(s) or lights connected to your computer via a LEGO WeDo hub.
