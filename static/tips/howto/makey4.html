Try It!

Touch the metal ends of both wires to complete the circuit.

You should see the tiny green light on the MaKey MaKey light up. Your computer will think the space key was pressed!

Not working?

Make sure the clips are securely connected.

Make sure you are holding one wire connected to EARTH and one clip on a key port.

For more troubleshooting tips, see the MaKey MaKey how-to tips.
