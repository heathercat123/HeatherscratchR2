Move by Pressing a Key

Click the Events category. Drag out a when this key pressed block and snap it on top:

Now the cat will move every time you press the spacebar on your keyboard.

You can choose a different key from the dropdown menu.

Want to change how fast it moves? Type a different number into the move block. Now, draw a finish line
